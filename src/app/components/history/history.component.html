Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Art Gallery Mobile App. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect the following information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including the features you access, the time and duration of your sessions, and your device information.
  • Search History: Your search queries are stored locally on your device to improve your user experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To display relevant content and recommendations
  • To monitor the usage of our app
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

4. Data Storage

All data collected by the app is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal information to any external servers. Your search history and app usage data are stored in your device's local storage.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have collected about you
  • Request the deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of certain data collection practices

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
